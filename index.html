About

Hrishikesh is a Ph.D. scholar from IIT Bombay. He is a gold medalist of his M.tech batch of 2018 from NIT Arunachal Pradesh with a specialization in Manufacturing Engg. In his M.tech project, he worked on a novel EDM simulation model on successive single spark EDM micro-machining.

He is currently working on SERB funded project titled 'Development of TiO2 nanotubes (TNT) surfaces with tailored properties for biomedical applications'. His research interests include TNT growth investigation on metal alloys and freeform surfaces and advanced metallurgical characterization that includes FIB-SEM, EDS, XRD, CV, EIS, and Nanoindentation. Non-conventional micromachining is another area of his interest that focuses on manufacturing components with micron tolerances. He has interests in an interdisciplinary study that involves the study of electrochemistry, corrosion science, mechatronics, material characterization, and advanced imaging and spectroscopy techniques.

He is a passionate martial artist, nunchuck practitioner, pianist and mixologist. He has traveled almost over entire India and especially loves Northeast Indian cuisines. He can also make scrumptious barbeques and cocktails by the riverside!
